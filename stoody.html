Stoody

Project

WHAT IS STOODY

Stoody is an educational application to give college students life more accessible. This application is excellent for students, fresh graduates, students, employees, and even companies.

STOODY'S FEATURES

Stoody is an application designed to give college students life more accessible with several features, such as Stoody Scholar, Stoody Intern, Stoody Tutor, and Stoody Event.

WHY I CREATED THIS PROJECT

Stoody is a group project which consists of four members and created to complete the Entrepreneurship Subject and published in the BISANARA Festival (www.bisanara.com).

MY CONTRIBUTION TO THE PROJECT

My Contribution to this project was to conduct a brainstorming, design the application's flow, prototype, and implement all the design to the application's final design.
